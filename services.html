Civil Work

Civil Work is primarily that work which is fixed to the house and cannot be changed easily. It includes tiling, bathroom renovation, breaking any walls, fixing the kitchen platform, electrical work, etc. It is usually more messy and tedious compared to the other work such as furniture and kitchen. In the process of building, you can just ask the builder to put in tiles, fittings, etc. as per your choice. It also requires some scheduling as multiple teams have to work in parallel and in sync to get the work done. As in our case, since we bought an old flat we decided to a whole bunch of civil work to get a newer feeling to the place. Material was not ordered in time, some of it was defective and had to be returned, laborers disappeared.
