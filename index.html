What We Do

Our business operations can be broadly classified under the following heads

Civil Services

Civil Work is primarily that work which is fixed to the house and cannot be changed easily. It includes tiling, bathroom renovation, breaking any walls, fixing the kitchen platform, electrical work, etc. It is usually more messy and tedious compared to the other work such as furniture and kitchen. It also requires some scheduling as multiple teams have to work in parallel and in sync to get the work done. Civil work usually takes a long time to complete. It generates a lot of mess in the house which can affect existing furniture.

Mechanical Services

A transmission pipeline is a transportation system that aids in the movement of goods and other materials. These pipelines can be used in transmitting oil, sewage, and even water. Natural gas, liquid petroleum, and biofuels are among the most commonly transmitted materials in pipelines. On the other hand, pipelines are not all the same and they differ based on the goods that will be transported and the materials used in their design. What's more, oil pipelines are hidden in the ground to conceal them from the public. As such, most people do not know that there is a large network of pipelines beneath them.
